The GOATs of Anime According to ME

My Top 3 Anime of All Time

One Piece

Epic world-building and long-term storytelling, with powerful themes of friendship, freedom, and chasing dreams.

Naruto

Strong character development and emotional arcs that show growth, perseverance, and the value of bonds.

Demon Slayer

Stunning animation and intense fight scenes paired with a simple but heartfelt story about family and sacrifice.
